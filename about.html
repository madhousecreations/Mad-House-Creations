WHERE PASSION

Mad House Creations was founded with one vision—to create an institute where creativity meets technology. Our classrooms simulate real production environments so students gain practical industry experience before entering the professional world.

From animation to VFX, gaming, digital art and content creation, we help students build strong portfolios and successful careers.
